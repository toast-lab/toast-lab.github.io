<p>Recently, we have a paper accepted by <a href="https://hpdc.sci.utah.edu/2026/" target="_blank">the 35th ACM International Symposium on High-Performance Parallel and Distributed Computing (HPDC 2026)</a>. Our paper titled <u>Pome: Parallelizing I/Os and Computations for Efficient LSM-tree-based Data Storage</u> was nominated as one of three candidates for the Best Paper Award. During the conference, our paper eventually won the award.</p>


<p>This paper was solely done by Toast Labmates. It has been <a href="https://dl.acm.org/doi/10.1145/3806645.3807603" target="_blank">online</a>. The source code is also <a href="https://github.com/toast-lab/LSM-Pome" target="_blank">available</a>.</p>
